Why Choose Our System?

🛡️

Contactless Tracking

In today’s world, safety is a priority. Our system offers a completely hands-free attendance process. Students just need to look at the camera, and the AI handles the rest with instant voice feedback—no physical registers or biometric touch required.

📈

Real-Time Data Insights

Forget manual data entry at the end of the day. Our system automatically syncs every attendance record to a Live Dashboard. Admin can monitor daily logs, track entry times, and generate reports instantly with our integrated CSV-to-Web technology

📍

Anti-Proxy AI Verification

Our system uses advanced KNN (K-Nearest Neighbors) and Computer Vision to ensure that only registered individuals can mark their attendance. It provides 100% accuracy by verifying the unique facial features of each student, making proxy attendance impossible.
